API Management Portal Documentation

The API Managment Portal is divided into 5 main sections...

  • First Part - Register on the developer portal Here
  • Second Part - Sign in
  • Third Part - Browse Products
  • Fourth Part - Registering an Application
  • Fifth Part - Submitting an Application for Review

First Part - Log in to your Developer Portal

The process begins with you signing in. Once you register, an OTP is sent to your registered phone number, you are supposed to come back to this portal to Sign in See the screenshot below....

Once you are signed in, you are met with the page below...See screenshot below

Once you are signed in, you can browse our list of Products, see the Pricing page OR proceed to ...Registering an application

Third Part - Browse Products

Click the Products link to view the different categories of our products

Click Overview to view any of these products and see particularly the Test Product to have a feel of some of our Public API's

You can then go ahead to view the pricing page that gives you a list of our API Subscription pricing options tailored to every business use case and requirements...

Have you identified a particular product or API subscription plan you would like to subscribe to? go ahead to Register an application

Second Part - Registering an Application

So you're registered now, and you have identified particular products - paid API subscriptions - you would like to consume, you are now ready to start consuming our API's. If you are still not registered, go back here to register To register an application on your developer account to start consuming our API's, use the form here to register a new application. Please Ensure you are logged in

  1. Click the dropdown under your Logged in name
  2. CLick "My Profile"
  3. You are directed to a Profile page
  4. Register new application properties by clicking the Register application
  5. Fill out the details properly and upload an image of size 100 x 100 pixels
  6. Save the application properties for review

Second Part - Submitting an Application for Review

Once an application has been successfully registered onto your developer account, from step 2 above register You need to submit your application for review, Go to here to register a new application. Please Ensure you are logged in

  1. Go to my profile
  2. At the My Profile page, you will see 3 things - Profile, Your subscription and particularly you will see the list of Applications registered to your account under Your applications If you need to make some modifications to the Application registered, Click the Edit To submit the application for review by the API Vendors, CLick the Submit button See the Image Below
